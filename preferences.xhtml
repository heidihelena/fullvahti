FullVahti looks up free, legal copies via Unpaywall and PubMed Central. Those services ask for a contact email (sent only to them). Optional: your library's OpenURL resolver. When set, the report adds a “Find in my library” link to each item with no open-access copy, so you can fetch a licensed copy through your library. FullVahti never downloads paywalled PDFs — it only hands the citation to your library.
